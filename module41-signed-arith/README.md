## Outcomes

After this module you can explain and practice the ideas taught by **`signed-arith`**, in the browser and/or on paper.

## Two tracks (pick one or both)

### Track A — Workbook (hands-on)

1. Open [EXAMPLES.md](EXAMPLES.md) and work the paper / sketch prompts.
2. Complete [CHECKLIST.md](CHECKLIST.md) without relying only on the UI.
3. Optional self-check: `./scripts/module.sh 41 --check` (from course root).

### Track B — Browser lab (online)

1. Local: [http://127.0.0.1:8080/tools/signed-arith/index.html](http://127.0.0.1:8080/tools/signed-arith/index.html)
2. Live: [https://universal-verification-methodology.github.io/learning/tools/signed-arith/](https://universal-verification-methodology.github.io/learning/tools/signed-arith/)
3. Load the **starter example**, then work challenges.
4. Check off the Track B items in [CHECKLIST.md](CHECKLIST.md).

> Concept labs are literacy tools — they prepare you for RTL courses; they do not replace writing synthesizable HDL.
